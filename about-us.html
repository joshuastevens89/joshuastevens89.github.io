About Me

Hi, my name is Joshua Stevens, and I am from Dallas, TX. I created a website called My Avatar and Me, using HTML, CSS, and GitHub Pages. This project allowed me to explore web development and learn the processes involved in designing and deploying a website.

Through this journey, I learned how to structure content with HTML, style it with CSS, and use GitHub Pages for hosting. Each step helped me develop new skills and fueled my passion for technology.

I aspire to build a career as a software engineer, creating innovative solutions and contributing to exciting projects. My work on My Avatar and Me is just the beginning, and I am eager to continue growing and exploring the world of software development.
